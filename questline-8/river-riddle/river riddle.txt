Initially the farmer, the wolf, the goat and the cabbages are in east end of the river.

Step 1: Farmer transports the goat to the west side of the river, leaving the wolf and the cabbage at the east side. And returns back to the east side alone.

Step 2: He next transports the wolf to the west side, and returns with the goat to the east side. 

Step 3: He takes the cabbages to the west side and returns alone to the east side.

Step 4: He now transport himself and the wolf to the west side of the river.

Thus, the wolf, the cabbages, the goat, and the farmer have reached the west side.
